“Love is patient, love is kind, but weddings are a chaotic whirlwind of emotions, messed up budgets, too many alterations, and stressful conversations. The best day of your life has the potential to turn you into an anxious mess. Fortunately, I have had plenty of experience dealing with that. Whether it was a friend's wedding, a relatives’ mehndi, or my own brother’s reception, I always found myself running errands, styling and restyling outfits for people, helping wedding parties accessorize to coordinate, and setting themes for different events which always stood out. I realized that my strength was organizing, styling, and keeping calm in a storm, while sticking to a set budget. That is everything you will ever need from your personal wedding stylist. All I ever wanted was my love for fashion and weddings to have a bigger purpose, and that is how Style my Aisle came into being. I truly believe that every bride and groom deserve to feel amazing on their wedding day, which is why I take on their fashion and styling worries, while they concentrate on creating a marriage that lasts forever.”

Vani Rajput
Wedding Stylist and Designer

OUR SERVICES

> WEDDING SOCIAL MEDIA

Capture the magic of your big day in every frame with our Wedding Social Media Services. We specialize in curating stunning, shareable content that showcases the elegance, romance, and joy of your wedding. From enchanting behind-the-scenes moments to exquisite bridal details, we’ll craft a captivating narrative that resonates with your online audience. Our team of experienced bridal stylists understands the unique beauty of weddings and knows how to translate it into visually captivating stories. Elevate your wedding experience by entrusting us to create unforgettable posts, stories, and highlights that encapsulate the essence of your celebration. Let us turn your special moments into a mesmerizing digital journey that you, your loved ones, and your followers will cherish forever.

> PERSONAL SHOPPING/ TROUSSEAU SHOPPING

Wedding shopping is one of the most difficult, emotional and potentially stressful parts of organizing wedding nuptials. Here, SMA acts as your fashion advocate & helps you verbalize the style you are looking for and assist in making your vision a reality.

>> VIRTUAL SHOPPING

Saving you time, energy & bringing you multiple options on your mobile’s screen, SMA team hunts for outfits & accessories & help you virtually shop, keeping you energized & stress free.

>> SHOPPING FOR FRIENDS & FAMILY

The friends & family who want to coordinate their outfits for pictures, give your budget & the wedding theme, the SMA team would curate a picture-perfect look for you.

>> SHOPPING FOR INTERNATIONAL CLIENTS

Brides & Grooms who are coming to India to get married or want to buy Indian outfits to fly back & wear at their weddings, SMA assists you plan your shopping itinerary, appointments & fittings for a hassle free experience.

> BOOKING FOR APPOINTMENTS

From scheduling your outfit trials to managing the wedding services like Mehendi booking, DJ, choreographer, decorators-- everything is taken care of here by SMA’s team.

> STYLING & RESTYLING

If you want to revamp your mom’s trunk & wear some outfits with which you have an emotional connection, for your wedding, SMA restyles that outfit, according to your style and trend, without losing its original charm. So, emotional connection + budget friendly - Isn’t that perfect for the millennial brides!

FOR INTERNATIONAL CLIENTS

Clients traveling to India for their wedding or wedding shohping, SMA plans your shopping itinerary according to your timings. Also, a proper virtual shopping tour is planned for the clients who cannot travel to India, but wish to dress in India attires.

The team at SMA curates your look & does the shopping for you, with you, virtually.
