About Us

“Whenever there was a wedding in my home, or a relative’s home or even at a friend’s place… I somehow, always found myself in the middle of running errands here and there. Helping everyone out with the outfits, accessories, adding a little bit of extra glam that I could, for the bride and groom. It always felt more than just an errand, and made me immensely happy. In Fact, I realized that a lot of people had informally ‘consulted’ me for their outfits choices, to go along with for shopping and other wedding shenanigans. That’s when I realized that my love for Fashion & Weddings had a bigger purpose.”

Vani Rajput
Wedding Stylist and Designer
